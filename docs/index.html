Research Interests

I am a PhD student supervised by Professor Katja Hose and Assistant Professor Matteo Lissandrini. My research area is in semantic data lakes with a particular focus on scalability. My goal is to achieve a highly scalable semantic data lake that is able to retreive high-quality datasets using data discovery and exploration enabling exemplar queries..
